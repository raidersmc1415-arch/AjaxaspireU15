Our Game Model

We play proactive, attacking football. We build from the back, press high out of possession, and value intelligence and bravery on the ball. Our system relies on technical execution and constant communication.

  • In Possession: Expand the field. Look to break lines. Be brave in 1v1 situations in the final third.
  • Out of Possession: Immediate counter-press upon losing the ball. Stay compact and deny central penetration.
  • Transition: React instantly. Find the highest open player when winning the ball. Sprint to recovery positions when losing it.
